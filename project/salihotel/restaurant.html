Food at Sali Hotel

Ivory Coast is very unique as far as delicious foods. Let's enjoy good foods!

Attieke

Attieke is made of delicious fried fish or Chicken for lunch or diner. We use local cassava also called yuka to make the cereal. Fried seasoned fish or chicken is added up to the meal as a compliment. You also have traditional drinks made of ginger or hibiscus flower. You will have organic fruits such as banana, pineaple and much more. A local desert made of yogurt and millet called "degue" is served.

Foutou Banane

Enjoy Foutou banane in our restaurant. Foutou banane is a traditional meal made of plantain banana. It is delicious with sauce made of palm oil with smoke or fresh fish and any type of meat. You will appreciate this famous recipe 100% made in Ivory Coast.

Alloco

Alloco is made of Fried plantain banana. Most people eat some alloco around 04PM as a snack. It is delicious with some egg or fried fish. Alloco is an interesting food.
