Unforgettable Experience in Abidjan

Enjoy your stay in a beautiful hotel. Sali Hotel offers an unforgettable experience to its valuable clients. The hotel is located in 'Abidjan', the capital of Ivory Coast. This beautiful city is surrounded by lagoons, the Atlantic ocean and a beautiful landscape composed of a wide variety of plants. The city is commonly known as the pearl of lagoons because of its uniqueness and surrounding of lagoons. Have a nice stay and Akwaba 'welcome'.

Discover beautiful beaches in the south of the Ivory Coast. Explore delicious foods unique to the country. Visit primary forests in the west of the country secured in national parks. Explore beautiful mountains in the north as well as in the west. Explore a breathtaking waterfall in the west of the country. Ivory Coast attracts thousands of tourists from all over the world.
